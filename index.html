About Us

Two friends who share two passions: Motorcycles and Ireland. Two Wheels Tours was founded by Alex and Vinny in 2020 and since then we have clocked hundreds of thousands of miles, sharing our experiences on two wheels with people all over the world. We are both trained in basic motorcycle maintenance and repairs on the roadside and we are both FBoS certified (First Bike on Scene - Emergency Response Skills).
